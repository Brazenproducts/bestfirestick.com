About BestFirestick

Independent streaming device reviews since 2026

Home › About

We started BestFirestick because we were tired of streaming device "reviews" that are just rewritten press releases. You know the ones — they list every spec from the product page, say "it's great for the price," and slap an affiliate link at the bottom. No real opinion, no real testing, no real help.

We wanted something different. A site that actually tells you which streaming device to buy and — just as importantly — which ones to skip. With opinions. Real ones.

How We Review

Every device we review gets used in a real home, on a real TV, for real daily streaming. Not a weekend test. Not a lab benchmark. We use these things the way you would — binge-watching shows, casting from phones, yelling at Alexa to turn on the TV while our hands are full.

We evaluate:

  • Daily usability — How does it feel to use every day?
  • Picture and sound quality — Does it actually deliver on the 4K/HDR/Atmos promises?
  • Voice control — Does the assistant actually understand you?
  • Reliability — Does it crash? Buffer? Need rebooting?
  • Value — Is it worth the money compared to alternatives?

Our Approach to Recommendations

We have opinions and we share them. If a device is overpriced, we'll say so. If a budget option punches above its weight, we'll celebrate it. We don't do the "every product is great in its own way" thing — some products are better than others, and we'll tell you which ones.

Affiliate Disclosure

Let's be transparent: some links on this site are Amazon affiliate links. When you buy something through our links, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site running.

Affiliate commissions do not influence our recommendations. We recommend the same products we'd buy with our own money. We don't accept free products from manufacturers. We don't do sponsored reviews. Our revenue comes from affiliate links and nothing else.
