Here's every device worth considering in 2026, ranked from best overall to most niche.

How We Picked These

We tested each device in real homes on real TVs. Not in a lab, not for a weekend — for months. We evaluated app loading speed, WiFi reliability, interface responsiveness, voice assistant accuracy, and the overall daily experience of living with each device.

We don't accept free products from manufacturers, and our rankings aren't influenced by affiliate commissions. We recommend what we'd actually buy with our own money.

For most people, the Fire TV Stick 4K Max is the one to get. It's the best balance of price, performance, and features. If you're on a tight budget, the Fire TV Stick Lite is unbeatable under $30.
